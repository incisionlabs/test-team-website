Most hospitals collect surgical data. Few convert it into actionable intelligence.

## The Data Gap

Operating rooms generate enormous amounts of data:
- Patient demographics and comorbidities
- Procedure types and durations
- Resource utilization patterns
- Complication and outcome metrics

Yet most institutions struggle to derive meaningful insights from this wealth of information.

## From Collection to Action

At IncisionLabs, we build systems that:

1. **Integrate** disparate data sources into unified views
2. **Analyze** patterns using advanced statistical methods
3. **Predict** outcomes and resource needs
4. **Recommend** evidence-based interventions

## Real Impact

A regional hospital system we work with identified through our analytics that:
- Thursday afternoon ORs had 23% higher turnover times
- Root cause: Specific instrument sterilization bottleneck
- Solution: Process redesign and staffing adjustment
- Result: 18% throughput increase, $1.8M annual savings
